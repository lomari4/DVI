Description

An ice monster has frozen the forest and has set out to plunge the entire world into an everlasting winter. Will you be able to stop it? Control the forest guardian and melt all the snow you see just by touching it. But be careful, you will have to avoid the monster's lackeys if you want to achieve your goal.

Basic Controls

When playing, click on the screen to enter or exit FullScreen

W to jump

A to move left

D to move right

SPACE to attack
